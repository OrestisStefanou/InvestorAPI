Description of other Ratings

Earnings Per Share (EPS) rating: compares a stocks last 2 quarters and 3 year EPS growth to all stocks. Ranks 1 to 99, with 99 the best. Rating of 90 means it outperformed 90% of all stocks.

Relative Strength (RS) rating: Stocks relative price change in last 12 months vs all stocks. Ranks 1 to 99. Best rating is 80 or more.

Sales+Profit+ROE (SMR) rating: combines sales, profit margins and return on equity into an A to E rating. ROE over 17% is preferred.

Accumulation / Distribution (Acc/Dis) rating: our proprietary price and volume formula shows if a stock is under accumulation (buying) or distribution (selling) in the last 3 months. A=buying, E=selling.

52 Week High is in blue if within 10% of its new high.

Vol % Change is volume traded yesterday vs average daily volume last 50 days. Vol % Chg. +50% & up are in blue.
