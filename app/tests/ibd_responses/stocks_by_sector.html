Earnings Per Share (EPS) rating: compares a stocks last 2 quarters and 3 year EPS growth to all stocks. Ranks 1 to 99, with 99 the best. Rating of 90 means it outperformed 90% of all stocks.

Relative Strength (RS) rating: Stocks relative price change in last 12 months vs all stocks. Ranks 1 to 99. Best rating is 80 or more.
